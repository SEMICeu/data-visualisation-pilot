Network visualisation of CORDIS data (H2020)

Through this page, you can view an interactive visualisation of CORDIS data as published by the Publications Office of the European Union Open Data Portal.
The datasets contain all projects funded by the European Union under the Horizon 2020 framework programme for research and technological development. This framework programme was funded and approved by the European Commission from 2014 to 2020.
The visualisation below shows the strength of collaboration between one central organisations (in orange) and its partners (in yellow). The thickness of the links represents the strength of the collaboration, i.e. the thicker the link, the more projets the two organisations collaborated on. The size of the bubble represents, for each organisations, the number of projects in which this organisation was involved at the European level.
