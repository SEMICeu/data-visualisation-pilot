Explore interactive visualisations of CORDIS as published by the Publications Office of the European Union Open Data Portal.

The datasets contain all projects funded by the European Union under FP6, FP7 and Horizon 2020 Framework Programmes for research and technological development. These framework programmes ran respectively from 2002 to 2006, 2007 to 2013, and 2014 to 2020.
